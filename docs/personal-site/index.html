About

Postdoctoral researcher at the University of Calgary specializing in snow hydrology, climate change impacts, and AI-augmented Earth-system modeling.

My research spans Arctic climate dynamics, glacier mass balance forecasting, and automated scientific discovery frameworks. I develop computational tools that bridge the gap between traditional hydrological modeling and modern machine learning approaches.

Currently working on SYMFLUENCE, a framework for coupling, calibrating, and running Earth-system models with unprecedented efficiency and reproducibility.

Research Projects
